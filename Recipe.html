Delicious Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 table spoon baking soda
  • 1/2 tablespoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter

Instructions

  1. In a bowl,mix flour,baking powder and salt
  2. In another bowl, beat egg with milk and melted butter
  3. Combine wet and ingredients and mix until smooth.
  4. Heat a skillet and pour in batter to form pancakes.
  5. Cook until bubbles form then flip and cook until golden.

Enjoy your homemade pancakes!
